GPU

The GPU, or graphics card, is dedicated to rendering images and videos. It enhances the computer's visual performance, making it crucial for gaming, video editing, and other graphics-intensive tasks.

Peripheral Devices

Peripheral devices, such as keyboards, mice, and printers, are external components that connect to the computer. They expand functionality and allow users to interact with and control the system for various tasks.

NIC

The NIC enables a computer to connect to a network, facilitating communication with other devices and access to the internet. It is essential for online activities, such as browsing, streaming, and online gaming.


CPU



The CPU, often referred to as the brain of the computer, executes instructions, performs calculations, and manages data, playing a pivotal role in the overall speed and functionality of the system.

RAM



RAM serves as temporary storage for actively used data and programs, allowing the computer to quickly access and manipulate information, contributing to the system's multitasking capabilities and overall responsiveness.

SSD



An SSD, in contrast to traditional hard drives, uses flash memory for data storage, providing faster read and write speeds, enhancing overall system performance, and significantly reducing loading times for applications and the operating system.



PSU

The PSU is responsible for converting electrical power from an outlet into a usable form for the computer's components. It ensures a stable and reliable power source, essential for the proper functioning of the entire system.

Motherboard

The motherboard is the main circuit board that houses the CPU, RAM, and other essential components. It provides connectivity and communication between different parts of the computer, serving as the foundation for the entire system.

Cooling System

The cooling system, including fans and heat sinks, helps regulate the temperature of the CPU and other critical components, preventing overheating and ensuring the stable operation of the computer over extended periods of use.
